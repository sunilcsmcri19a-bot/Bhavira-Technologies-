Transforming EPS waste into high-performance electrochemical electrodes through advanced plasma conversion technology

The Problem

Millions of tonnes of plastic waste, including Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), end up in landfills every year, creating long-term environmental damage while valuable material resources are lost. At the same time, advanced electrochemical technologies rely on costly and resource-intensive materials.

Our Technology Process

1. EPS Waste Collection

Collection and sorting of landfill-derived EPS waste materials

2. Plasma Conversion

Advanced plasma treatment for material transformation

3. Electrode Fabrication

Manufacturing of conductive electrodes with thermocol-derived carbon and graphite powder

4. Advanced Applications

Deployment in energy, sensing, and environmental systems

Key Applications

Battery Systems

High-performance electrodes for energy storage solutions

Metal Recovery

Efficient electrochemical metal recovery from waste streams

Water Treatment

Advanced electrochemical water purification systems

Electrochemical Sensors

Precision sensing for environmental and industrial monitoring

Wastewater Monitoring

Real-time detection and analysis of water contaminants

Biosensing

Medical and biological detection applications
