Our Solutions

At Bhavira Technologies, we specialize in converting EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) waste using advanced plasma conversion technology. Our systems are capable of recycling over 500kg of waste per week, resulting in a 95% reduction in CO2 emissions.

Each month, our processes handle over 5000kg of waste and produce more than 100,000 units of high-quality products.

We create thermocol-derived carbon and graphite powder composites that serve numerous applications, including Battery Systems, Metal Recovery, Water Treatment, Electrochemical Sensors, Wastewater Monitoring, and Biosensing.
