The Problem

Millions of tonnes of plastic waste end up in landfill every year, creating long-term environmental damage while valuable material resources are lost. At the same time, advanced electrochemical technologies rely on costly and resource-intensive materials.

Our Solution

Waste-to-Value Processing

We convert landfill-derived plastic waste into functional polymer composite electrodes using scalable and sustainable processing techniques.

High-Performance Electrodes

Our electrodes are designed for electrochemical sensing, energy storage, and environmental applications without relying on expensive raw materials.

Scalable & Industry-Ready

The technology is compatible with roll-to-roll manufacturing and existing electrochemical systems, enabling rapid translation to industry.

Impact

Environmental

Diverts plastic from landfill, reduces reliance on virgin materials, and lowers the carbon footprint of electrode manufacturing.

Scientific & Technical

Enables next-generation electrochemical sensors, energy systems, and circular-economy materials.

Economic

Creates a pathway for low-cost, high-value products from waste streams relevant to industry and investors.

Who We Work With

BT is an academic spin-out working with research institutions, investors, industry partners, and customers to accelerate sustainable materials from lab to market.
